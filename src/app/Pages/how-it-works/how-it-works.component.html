Fruitastic is a passion driven company which aims to deliver garden-fresh fruit and veggies to consumers all across Victoria. From day one our mantra has remained unchanged, which is to deliver premium quality fruits and vegetables with the fastest possible turnaround from farm to kitchen to all fellow Victorians. Fuelled by the overwhelming response of our patrons we are constantly striving for excellence. Fruitastic is as real as it gets, we are a customer centric company which always keeps its customers first. Our lightning fast home delivery service ensures that each customer receives fresh and mouth-watering fruits, like the ones displayed on our website. We are early risers and usually kick start our operations in early morning hours. Over the course of 15 years we have developed strong working ties with Premium Australian farms which help us deliver quality products at your door step. Fruitastic staunchly believes in sustainability and we are playing an active role in preserving our ecosystem as we only partner up with farms following sustainable farming methods. Our carefully crafted pictures help you navigate your way through our website, but still if you feel the need of assistance don't hesitate at all, simply click the Live Chat link and allow us to clear out any ambiguities.
